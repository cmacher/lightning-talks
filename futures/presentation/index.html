Scala
Futures

Futures allow us to

  • perform operations in parallel
  • efficiently
  • in a non-blocking way

Futures

  • are placeholder objects for a result that doesn't yet exist
  • are computed concurrently and their results can be later collected
  • lead to faster, asynchronous, non-blocking parallel code

Advantages

  • callbacks instead of typical blocking operations
  • composition of Futures
    • with combinators 
      map, flatMap, and filter
    • or with for-comprehensions (syntactic sugar)
